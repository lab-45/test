    Not use "I don't know"

    Instead of use I don't know better use the next senteces:

    When I don't have a lot of information to share or don't really want to share the information, I can to use something like that:

    • I have no idea
    • No idea
    • I have no clue
    • not a clue
    • no clue
    • beats me
    • how should i know?
    • Don't ask me

    When I can get some information, I can to use something like this:

    • I'm not sure
    • I'm not sure, but I can find out
    • Your guess is as good as mine
    • Who knows?
    • I wish a knew

    Si no sabe la respuesta pero va a tratar de encontrarla (más común en el trabajo)

    If I really don't have the answer but I can try to find out, I can to use something like that:

    • I'll get back to you on that

    Si con la información que tengo no puedo respoder la pregunta, entonces se puede utilizar:

    If with the information that I have, I can't answer, I can to use the next sentece:

    • Not as far as I know

    And finally I could combine phrases, like that:

    • I'm not sure, but I'll try to find out
    • I'm not sure, but I'll double-check
    • I'm not sure, but let me look into it
    • I'm not sure, but I'll check
    • I'm not sure, let me find out, and I'll get back to you
    • I'm not sure, let me find out, and I'll let you know

    Como truco, si no se del todo la respuesta podría utilizar:

    That's a good question
